This is where trained models of DEP-GAN (i.e., generator and critics) are stored.

Trained weights of the DEP-GAN's generator are also provided.
 - PROB stands for probability, where the DEP-GAN was trained by using probability map (PM) of WMH.
 - IM stands for irregularity map, where the DEP-GAN was trained using irregularity map (IM) of WMH.
 - noSL stands for no stroke lesions, in which no stroke lesions included in the training.

Trained weights of the DEP-UResNet are also provided now.
